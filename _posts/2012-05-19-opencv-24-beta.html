--- date: 2012-05-19 07:01:25 layout: post permalink: opencv-24-beta.html published: true short: After the long 9 months since 2.3.1 release, but just after a few weeks since 2.4 beta, we are happy to announce the release of OpenCV v2.4.0, the latest and the greatest version of the library! title: OpenCV v2.4 released ---

OpenCV team has just released the latest and greatest OpenCV release v2.4! Congratulations to everybody who took part in preparing the release and/or contributed new functionality, submitted bug reports, and everybody else who waited for it! :)

We put a lot of efforts to make it one of the most stable OpenCV releases, over 300 issues have been resolved in the past few months (http://code.opencv.org/projects/opencv/roadmap). Besides the numerous bug fixes, here are the major highlights of the release: http://code.opencv.org/projects/opencv/wiki/ChangeLog. Everything, except for the packages themselves, is now hosted on our brand-new developer site http://code.opencv.org.
