Today OpenCV crosses important milestone: five million downloads!

5 000 000!

With an average of over three thousands downloads per day, the demand for OpenCV only continues to grow as researchers across the globe tackle tough problems of machine perception. We congratulate all OpenCV developers and the many contributors that continue to make OpenCV a success.

Thank you all for supporting and trusting us!
