We thank to our users who found a critical error in 2.4.6 version: camera pipeline for Linux (V4L) doesn't work. We have created a fix for this problem in 2.4 branch and have published 2.4.6.1 release for Linux with it.

More details is in the discussion on the OpenCV Q&A forum.

OpenCV Development Team.
