At this year's ICCV conference, Gary Bradski accepted the Mark Everingham Prize on behalf of the OpenCV Team. This award commemorates Mark Everingham and his contributions to the Computer Vision community and is granted in the same spirit by the IEEE PAMI TC.

Thank you to all the contributors of OpenCV, past and present, in making this possible and congratulations to all!
