Cassandra is a pioneering visual programming tool and development platform for modeling algorithms, typically used in image, signal and video processing; its flexible plug-in concept allows simple extensions. The new 11.5 version offers you a broad range of exciting new features.

Cassandra 11.5’s unique selling points:

  • Features more than 100 new stations to enhance Cassandra’s modular structure
  • Covers an impressive range of bug fixes, encompassing more than 50 different varieties
  • Equipped with an integrated Kinect plug-in for depth-enhanced images
  • An additional 3D charts plug-in rounds off this innovative software
